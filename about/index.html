About Me

I grew up in Indore, India, which is now the cleanest city in the country. I am incredibly proud of that. Growing up, I was always drawn to figures like Alexander the Great, Johan Liebert, Sasuke Uchiha, Griffith, Augustus Caesar, and many others like them. What I admire most is how disciplined they all were. Beyond that, I believe humans are amazing, the world is amazing, and I always say that I love everyone and everything. I truly mean it.

I love classical music, I loved playing God of War, and I’m a sucker for good movies. But more importantly, I want to start creating things that have a big impact. That’s why I set up this website. It actually took me nearly a full day just to get it running. Here, I’ll be documenting my daily tasks, thoughts, and goals. In a way, this site is a portal into my inner world.

I look forward to growing, preferably together. I’d really appreciate it if you connect with me on my socials.
