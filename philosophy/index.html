May 17th, 2024 - 14:30

As I was sitting at my desk in the office, I started wondering what time it would be in India. From there, I thought, “Hey, it’s just the Earth rotating on its axis and nothing else. It’s just cause and effect.” Then I thought to myself that time is just an illusion. OR MAYBE I AM JUST NOT SMART ENOUGH TO ACCEPT IT. However, I was thinking that it would be incredibly difficult to communicate the position of an object, say near Jupiter, to people in two different places, one on Earth, and the other somewhere very close to a black hole where time essentially slows down. Okay, sure, XYZ coordinates, yes, but since time is slowed down for the people near the black hole, what would you tell them? How would you convey that to them?
